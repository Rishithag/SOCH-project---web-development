PROBLEM STATEMENT

In some areas people are suffering with kidney diseases, to overcome this problem is there any preventive measures to adopt before supplying the water to the customer

DESCRIPTION

To find the measures that could be taken which could help prevent people from suffering through kidney and other diseases due to improper supply of water to the consumers in many parts of India

SOLUTION / APPROACH

Our solution to this problem is to build an android app which will tell whether the water is portable or not by means of a sensor(Arduino) which will calculate the toxicity of water ,conductivity of water and according to the data outputs given by the sensor, the app will tell whether the water being supplied is portable or not. Our app will provide information of water treatment methods which will be an add on and queries regarding any preventive measure can be known using a chatbot linked with the app database.

SOLUTION IN DETAIL

We will create our own conductivity sensor using Arduino which will gather data regarding the pollution levels of the water.

TOOLS USED TO MAKE THE SENSOR

  • Arduino uno
  • USB cable
  • 9 volt battery
  • 9 volt battery connector
  • Disposable pen
  • Electrical tape
  • Nichrome wire
  • Computer with internet access

TECHNOLOGY STACK

  • Android Studio---> A platform to develop an Android Application.
  • Dialogflow---> A platform to develop chatbots.
  • Arduino Programming Environment---> A platform to make the Arduino work by writing a code for obtaining the desired data.
